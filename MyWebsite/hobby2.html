Learning Japanese

I first picked up learning the Japanese language back when I was in middle school. I didn't get far - only learning the first 2 alphabets and some grammar points. However when I attended Auburn for undergrad, I enrolled in Japanese classes in hopes of learning again. I enjoyed the classes very much and ended up getting a minor in Asian Studies with a concentration in Japanese. Unfortunately after graduating, I did not get many opportunities to practice Japanese so I had lost much of it. Fortunately in 2025, I had an amazing opportunity to go to Japan with my friends, and this renewed my interest in Japanese since I was able to use my skills there. Since then, I have began studying again and hope to take the JLPT test this year.
